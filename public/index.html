Why Karya

India needs its own inference layer.

Indian companies should be able to build serious AI products without sending every prompt abroad or paying frontier-model prices for routine work. Karya is built for that reality.

What actually matters in production?

  • Model quality that is reliable enough for the business task, not leaderboard chasing for every request.
  • Inference cost that stays predictable when pilots become real products with real traffic.
  • Low latency for coding, agents, support, and everyday AI workflows where speed shapes adoption.
  • India-hosted data control, practical model guidance, and a local team accountable for tuning the workload.
Product thesis

Most enterprise AI does not need the largest model.

It needs the right model, tuned well, served close to the data, and cheap enough to run repeatedly. That is where optimized open-source inference wins.

Comparison

Use the right lane for the workload.

Model routers like OpenRouter are useful for model choice and experimentation. Karya is focused on a dedicated India-hosted inference lane where cost, latency, and data residency matter.

OpenRouter-style model routers

Great for trying many models through one API. Public prices, latency, and throughput vary by model and upstream provider.

Karya optimized lane

Open-source models on Indian GPU infrastructure, starting at ₹5/M tokens, targeting 2-3x faster inference and 2-3x lower cost.

Frontier direct APIs

Best for the hardest, highest-value tasks. Often worth reserving for requests where premium accuracy justifies premium cost.
